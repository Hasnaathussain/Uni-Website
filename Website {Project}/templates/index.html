About the Department

The Department of Electronics at Quaid-i-Azam University is a leading center for education and research in electronics. We offer undergraduate (BS) and postgraduate (MS, MPhil, PhD) programs, and our faculty are actively involved in cutting-edge research.

Our mission is to provide high-quality education and foster research excellence in the field of electronics. We aim to produce skilled professionals and researchers who can contribute to the technological advancement of society.

To be recognized as a globally renowned institution in electronics education and research, making significant contributions to the field and addressing the challenges of the future.
